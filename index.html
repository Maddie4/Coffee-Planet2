Our goal is to help more people experience the finest global arabica coffee

We first started our coffee venture in 2005, when the local coffee-landscape in the UAE was very different and somewhat ordinary. With the goal to help people experience the finest global Arabica coffee, we set out to look at how coffee was being made, who was growing what and how we could develop our own great tasting blends. Launching Coffee Planet on the Arabian Peninsula made perfect sense to us, since what better place to learn from the past 500 years of Arabica coffee roasting and apply our modern approach.


The Roastery

We have the capacity to roast over 100 tonnes of coffee per month alongside our coffee capsule production line. Our Roastmaster has the acute ability to discover the subtle nuances of coffee flavors,fragrances, aromas and tastes from one cup to another, yhanks to their coffee Q-grade qualification.
